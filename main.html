About

Hello, my name is Dymov Volodymyr. I am 14 years old, and I live in Ukraine all my life. When a full-scale war began in Ukraine, I decided to record tracks to release all my anger at Russia. My first track was "My Flag". In the first half of the track, I wrote about the Ukrainian flag, how dear it is to us and that it must remain so. In the second half, I addressed Russia, urging it to hear us and understand that we are the same people as they are (if that is the case) and that we want to live in peace. I know three languages: Ukrainian, English, and Chinese. In my free time, I listen to music, scroll through TikTok, or hang out with friends.

Listen to my tracks on my YouTube channel.
